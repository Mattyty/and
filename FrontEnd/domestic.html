Domestic Services


We understand that the decision to renovate your home isn’t taken lightly, and here at A & D we’ve got you covered! From a full domestic refurbishment to a newly plastered wall, all jobs receive the same attention to detail and quality craftsmanship that can build value into your property.


Polite and experienced, our expert team are always aware that we’re in your home, and employ a customer-focused professional approach to allow you to feel comfortable whether you chose to vacate the premises during the refurbishment or not.

Full Domestic Refurbishments


A & D complete full domestic refurbishments to the highest quality; from the planning stages right through to the finished product, you’ll have peace of mind along the way. Project management, planning permissions and building regulations can be safely left in our hands, and can be discussed at the time of quotation.

Our dedicated team of subcontractors whom we know and trust are fully-certified and can be relied upon for a professional finish. Working alongside you is imperative to achieving a unique and superior result which matches your lifestyle, and our project foreman will be available to liaise as your point of contact.

A domestic refurbishment can involve anything from re-plastering and decorating, to a new kitchen and bathroom or new external windows and doors. We also offer all aspects of joinery and carpentry including door hanging and framing, skirting boards and boarding out lofts.

Property Maintenance
