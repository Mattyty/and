Commercial Services


A&D Commercial Interiors Ltd design and install suspended ceilings and partitioning systems, and also specialise in refurbishment and maintenance of commercial areas.


We pride ourselves on delivering projects to the highest standards, and all of our work is completed by friendly and professional staff with minimal disruption. Our company has over 15 years experience working on general commercial properties throughout the UK.

Our services include:

  • Suspended ceiling design - new and maintenance
  • Metal M. F. Ceilings ready for plaster
  • All aspects of partitioning system
  • All electrical work with our own in house electrician
  • Full project management to work within the time and budget restraints
  • Lighting design & layout to the agreed specification
  • Shop fitting carried out to required specification
  • Air Conditioning incorporated into the ceiling layout
  • All aspects of flooring including laminate floors, carpet tiles and Antico flooring
  • Any works of Refurbishment and Maintenance
  • All aspects of joinery work
  • Night work undertaken if requested
